Редакциялық стандарттар

Our commitment to accuracy, fairness, and transparency guides every story we publish.

Fact-Checking Process

Every story undergoes rigorous verification before publication. We require multiple independent sources for breaking news and maintain a database of verified contacts across government, business, and civil society.

  • Minimum two independent sources for breaking news
  • Direct quotes verified with original speakers
  • Statistical data cross-referenced with official sources
  • Expert opinions balanced with opposing viewpoints

Source Verification

We maintain strict protocols for source verification and protection, ensuring both accuracy and safety for those who provide information in the public interest.

  • Identity verification for all quoted sources
  • Secure communication channels for sensitive information
  • Anonymous source protection protocols
  • Regular review of source credibility and track record

Correction Policy

We promptly correct errors and clearly communicate changes to our readers. Transparency in corrections builds rather than undermines credibility.

  • Corrections published within 24 hours of identification
  • Clear explanation of what was corrected and why
  • Original error preserved in correction notice
  • Social media corrections for shared content

Ethical Guidelines

Our journalists adhere to the highest ethical standards, avoiding conflicts of interest and maintaining independence from political and commercial pressures.

  • Financial disclosure for business coverage
  • Political neutrality in election reporting
  • Respect for privacy in personal matters
  • Fair representation of all viewpoints

How We Work

Transparency in our newsroom processes builds trust and understanding with our readers.

Editorial Meetings

Daily editorial meetings at 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM to discuss story priorities, breaking news response, and resource allocation.

  • • Morning: Breaking news and day's priorities
  • • Afternoon: Next day planning and story review
  • • Weekly: Long-term investigations and features

Publication Workflow

Multi-stage review process ensures accuracy and quality before publication, with different protocols for breaking news and feature content.

  • • Reporter draft and fact-check
  • • Editor review and verification
  • • Copy editing and legal review
  • • Final approval and publication

Breaking News Protocol

Rapid response system for breaking news while maintaining accuracy standards through streamlined verification processes.

  • • 15-minute initial alert publication
  • • Continuous updates with new information
  • • Full story within 2 hours
  • • Follow-up analysis within 24 hours

Reader Engagement

Active engagement with reader feedback, story suggestions, and community input to ensure our coverage serves public interest.

  • • Weekly reader feedback review
  • • Monthly community forums
  • • Quarterly editorial board meetings
  • • Annual reader survey and response
